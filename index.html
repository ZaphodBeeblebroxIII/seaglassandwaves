Welcome to my website! Here you can find my beautiful jewellery and art made from sea glass that I collect from beaches along the south coast of England. I started collecting sea glass in 2017 and it quite quickly became a bit of an obsession! I love being on the beach, and love collecting the different pieces of sea glass. I have acquired quite a collection and enjoy making the pieces into jewellery and pictures. Feel free to browse my shop, and I hope you like my creations! Lynn x
